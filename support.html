Frequently asked questions

How does NameAtlas find names?

Enter a meaning — like “moon,” “brave,” or “joy” — and NameAtlas shows how that meaning becomes a name across 100+ languages, each with its pronunciation and a rarity badge.

Why do I need an internet connection?

Translations are generated live, so an internet connection is required to discover names. Your saved favorites, shortlist, and history remain available on your device.

How is a name's rarity decided?

Each name is checked against a list of common given names and labeled Rare, Uncommon, or Common to help you spot the truly distinctive ones. It's a guide, not an official ranking.

How do I hear a name pronounced?

Tap the speaker icon on any result, or open a name and tap “Hear it.” NameAtlas speaks it in that language's voice.

Where are my favorites and history stored?

Entirely on your device. NameAtlas has no account and doesn't upload your data. Deleting the app removes them.

How do I report a problem?

In the app, open Settings → Contact Developer to send us an email with a short diagnostics log attached, or email pixieface92@gmail.com directly.
